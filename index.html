Who sang with whom, when?

When did two or three singers attend the same singing? This uses the data from Fasola.org to match singers to the singings they have attended, and which ones they attended together. Currently the data is from 1995-2022.

Enter the names of the leaders you want to see, one on each line.

For example, if you want to see all the singings that both David Ivey and David Lee were at, enter David Ivey followed by David Lee.

Then, press the Get shared singings button.
